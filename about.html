The Story

The Work Has Always Been the Same

Anthony has spent more than thirty years walking into rooms that needed changing and leaving them better than he found them. From construction sites in Saudi Arabia to corporate boardrooms in Houston to youth programs in Florida, the work has always been the same: find the genius in the room and give it permission to show up.

He discovered his life's purpose in his thirties by doing something simple: he looked back at every job he'd ever held and asked what he loved most. The answer was always the same: developing people. He named it "Unleashing Genius" and has been proving it ever since.

In June 2026, he retired from Bechtel after a distinguished career as an executive leadership coach and workshop facilitator, and moved to Florida to build the next chapter: three tracks of coaching work built around the people and communities he's always been heading toward.

Along the way, he volunteered at Covenant House, sitting with young people the system had written off. He stepped inside correctional facilities for conversations most people are afraid to have. He's coached C-suite executives who had everything except someone willing to tell them the truth.

The thread running through all of it: every person he sat across from had more in them than they'd been allowed to see. His job was always to help them see it.

The Philosophy

What He Believes. How He Works.

  • 🎯

    Honesty over comfort

    Growth lives on the other side of a hard conversation. Anthony doesn't traffic in comfortable. He trades in honest, delivered with enough care that people can actually use it.

  • 🤝

    The work is personal, or it's nothing

    He's sat in enough workshops where nobody changed. The difference is always the same: did the facilitator actually care about this person? Anthony does. Every time.

  • 💡

    Everyone has genius in them

    Not potential. Not promise. Actual genius, specific to who they are. His job is to help them find it, trust it, and use it. That's it.

  • 👁

    Full presence is the work

    When someone knows you're actually in the room with them, not just performing, everything changes. Anthony is present or he's not there.

"When I discovered that unleashing people's genius was my purpose, not in a workshop, but by looking back across every job I'd ever held and seeing the same pattern, I stopped wondering what I was supposed to do with my life."
