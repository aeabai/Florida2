One adult who believes in a kid can change the trajectory of a life. I've seen it. I'm doing it again.

Who It Reaches

Young People Who've Been Written Off

Anthony spent years at Covenant House. He knows what it looks like when the system has given up on a kid — and he knows what happens when one adult refuses to give up with it.

This track reaches young people between the ages of 14 and 24 who are navigating circumstances most adults would find overwhelming:

  • Youth experiencing housing instability or homelessness
  • Young people aging out of the foster care system
  • At-risk teens in underserved Florida communities
  • Young people involved with the juvenile justice system
  • Youth in group homes, shelters, and transitional programs
  • First-generation students needing mentorship and direction

They don't need a pep talk. They need a person — someone who shows up, doesn't flinch, and tells them the truth about what they're capable of.

The Program

Built for Real Life. Not Theory.

👥

Group Sessions

Facilitated group programs focused on identity, leadership, choices, and what's actually possible. Real conversations — not workbook exercises. Anthony leads these with the same presence and directness he brings to the boardroom.

  • 6–20 participants per cohort
  • Multi-week program design
  • Adaptable curriculum
🤝

One-on-One Mentorship

Individual mentorship for young people who need focused one-on-one attention. Not therapy. Not case management. Just a consistent adult who shows up, listens, challenges, and helps them think about the future differently.

  • Regular sessions over 3–6 months
  • Goal-setting and follow-through
  • Available in-person (Florida)
🏗

Program Design for Organizations

Anthony works directly with youth-serving organizations to design, build, and deliver leadership programming. If your organization is working with at-risk youth and needs a partner who knows how to do this work — let's talk.

  • Ongoing program facilitation
