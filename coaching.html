Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to be in Florida to work with you?
No. One-on-one coaching is available via video anywhere in the country. In-person sessions and workshops are available in Florida, and Anthony travels nationwide for intensives and organizational engagements.
What's the difference between coaching and therapy?
Coaching is forward-focused — it's about where you're going and what's in the way. Therapy works with the past and is clinically licensed. Anthony is a coach, not a therapist. If you need clinical support, he'll tell you that directly.
How long does a typical engagement last?
Most 1:1 clients work with Anthony for three to six months, though some continue longer. Workshops and intensives are self-contained. The right length depends on what you're working on — Anthony will be direct with you about what he thinks makes sense.
What if I'm not sure coaching is right for me?
Reach out. Anthony will have a straightforward conversation with you about whether this is the right fit — for both of you. He doesn't take on clients he doesn't think he can genuinely help.
