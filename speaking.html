Signature Talk

"Unleashing Genius: What Real Leadership Looks Like in Tough Industries"

This isn't a talk about motivation. It's a talk about what leadership actually requires — in construction sites, boardrooms, shelters, and cellblocks. It's built from thirty years of stories that most speakers don't have and couldn't tell.

Anthony talks about the leader he used to be — effective, results-driven, and leaving wreckage behind him. He talks about the conversation that changed him. And he walks audiences through the framework he's used to help thousands of people find and use their own genius.

It's honest. It's personal. And by the time it's over, people aren't just inspired — they have something they can actually use.

Themes Covered

  • What genius actually is — and why most organizations are wasting it
  • The difference between managing people and developing them
  • Why honesty is the most underrated leadership tool
  • What it takes to transform a reluctant team in days instead of months
  • How to create a culture where people can bring their actual best
  • Lessons from leading teams in Saudi Arabia, corporate America, and correctional facilities

Audience Outcomes

  • A clear definition of what "unleashing genius" actually means in practice
  • At least one hard question about their own leadership style
  • A concrete framework for identifying and developing the genius on their team
  • Permission to lead differently — more honestly, more personally
